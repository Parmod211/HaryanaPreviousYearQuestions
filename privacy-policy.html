📚 Haryana Previous Year Questions – Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Last Updated: April 2026

📋 Introduction

Welcome to Haryana Previous Year Questions, an educational app providing Haryana state exam question papers and study materials (e.g. HSSC, HPSC, CET), developed by educationhub. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

By using Haryana Previous Year Questions, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

📦 Information We Collect

  • Local Preferences — We may store your preferences and settings locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.
  • Advertising Data — We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data as per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • No Personal Registration — We do not require account creation, login, name, or password.

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or phone number
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties (except AdMob)
  • We do NOT have any user accounts or registration system
  • We do NOT sell your data to anyone
  • We do NOT upload any personal data to any server

🔒 App Permissions Explained

  • INTERNET — Required for loading PDF papers and displaying ads
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Required to send study reminders and notifications (optional, Android 13+)
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Required to re-schedule daily study reminders after device reboot

📢 Google AdMob & Advertising

Haryana Previous Year Questions uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including but not limited to:

  • Device advertising ID (GAID)
  • IP address and general location
  • Ad interaction and performance data
📌 Google's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

💾 Data Storage & Security

  • All preferences are stored locally on your device
  • You can clear all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from Settings
  • We use industry-standard security practices for all network connections (HTTPS)

👶 Children's Privacy

Haryana Previous Year Questions is an educational app intended for students preparing for state exams. It is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
